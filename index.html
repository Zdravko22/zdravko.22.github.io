About us

Z-watches is a company that was founded in 2003. We provide the best quality watches on the market. Our goal is to satisfy all the needs of our clients and show the world the quality of our watches.

The most popular watches

These are some of the best selling watches today

Rolex

Rolex is one of the most famous and popular watches on the planet. First of all, recognizable by the quality of production, then by its specific appearance. Due to the fact that it is one of the "best" watches on the planet, the prices alone reach incredible figures.

Casio

Casio is also a popular watch that is mostly known to young people. Mostly because of its price because it is very affordable. Specific appearance and very interesting production. One of the most expensive was sold at auction for $6,200. Highly respected watch in the world.

Seiko

Seiko, like Casio, is one of the more affordable models and also has a lot of fans of younger generations. They are very similar in price, but the construction and quality itself are quite different. Only one model sells for $500,000 and it's a special edition.
